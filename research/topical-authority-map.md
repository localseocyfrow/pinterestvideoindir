## 1. Methodology

Clusters were built from Ahrefs Keywords Explorer (matching terms + related terms for
the pillar) and cross-checked against the competitor's actual ranking pages
(Site Explorer → Top pages). Each cluster is mapped to **one** canonical URL to avoid
keyword cannibalization. Priority = search demand × ease (inverse KD) × strategic gap
(is the intent currently uncovered on our site or weakly covered by competitors).

## 6. Topical Authority & Internal Linking

- **Hub-and-spoke:** Pillar `/pinterest-video-indir/` links down to every tool spoke
  (galeriye, 4k, ücretsiz, indirici, mp4, filigransız) and to guide spokes; spokes link
  back up to the pillar and laterally to 1–2 sibling spokes.
- **Guides → tools:** Every informational guide (nasıl, yükleme, device pages) ends with a
  contextual CTA linking to the matching tool page.
- **Nav/footer:** Add the top new tool pages to footer "Araçlar"; keep primary nav lean.
- **Sitemap & llms.txt:** Register each new route in `src/data/routes.ts` (single source
  feeding sitemap.xml + llms.txt) at build time.
- **Schema:** Reuse `webAppSchema`, `breadcrumbSchema`, `faqSchema`; add page-specific
  `HowTo` schema to guide pages for AEO/AI-overview capture.

## 7. Content Quality Guardrails (non-thin, non-copy)

Every new page must include, minimum: unique H1 + intro tied to its specific intent, the
working downloader tool, a 3-step how-to, a differentiated feature/benefit block, a
device/quality/format nuance section unique to that cluster, and a page-specific FAQ set
(new QA entries in `src/data/faq.ts`, not reused verbatim). Target ≥ 400 meaningful words
of original Turkish copy. Do **not** replicate competitor phrasing, layout, or FAQ wording.
